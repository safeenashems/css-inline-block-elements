US-Israel attack on Iran Day 4 updates

As of March 3, 2026, the conflict involving Israel, the United States, and Iran has escalated into a significant regional war, now in its fourth day. The military operation, named “Epic Fury” by the Pentagon, began on February 28, 2026. Key Developments Death of Iranian Leadership: Joint U.S.-Israeli strikes have reportedly killed Iran’s Supreme Leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, and other top security officials. An interim leadership council has been formed in Tehran. Casualties: Iran: The Iranian Red Crescent reports at least 787 deaths nationwide. United States: U.S. Central Command (CENTCOM) confirmed 6 service members killed and 18 seriously wounded, primarily from a strike on a facility in Kuwait. Israel: At least 11 people have been killed in retaliatory Iranian missile strikes. Broadening Fronts: Lebanon: Israel has launched ground operations in southern Lebanon and conducted airstrikes in Beirut targeting Hezbollah. Regional Strikes: Iran has launched retaliatory drones and missiles at U.S. and allied assets in Saudi Arabia (Riyadh embassy), Kuwait, the UAE, and Bahrain. Economic Impact: Iran has threatened to close the Strait of Hormuz, a critical global oil route, causing oil prices to surge and global stock markets to plunge.
